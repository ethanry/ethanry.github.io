2/26/19 car of the day

1990's Chevrolet Tracker

The 1990's Chevrolet Tracker or "Geo Tracker" as some might know it is a mini-suv produced in Japan.

Although the car may appear compact, the tracker was certified as a light truck due to the off-road capabilities. The tracker has been produced by many other brands and countries how ever the Chevy edition in the 1990's is considered to be the best production models avaliable.

Interesting features

  • 1.6L SOCH 4-cylinder engine
  • 80 HP
  • All wheeldrive
